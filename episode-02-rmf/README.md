# Episode 2 lab: the Risk Management Framework, one system

> Pairs with the Field Notes article "RMF without the jargon."

Episode 1 gave you the wide view (the CSF profile). This episode zooms in on a single system and walks it through the seven RMF steps, from idea to a real sign-off.

## What is in here

- **rmf-walkthrough.md**: the story. The Customer Web Portal through all seven steps, in plain language. Read this alongside the article.
- **rmf-step-tracker.xlsx**: a simple tracker showing where the portal is in each step, with status and owners. This is what a small team actually keeps.

## Do this

1. Read [rmf-walkthrough.md](rmf-walkthrough.md) with the [system-boundary diagram](../company/diagrams/system-boundary.png) open.
2. Open the tracker. See how the portal is Done on Prepare and Categorize, In progress on Select and Implement, and Not started on Assess, Authorize, Monitor. That is a realistic mid-flight picture.
3. Notice the hand-offs to Episode 3: Categorize produces the FIPS 199 result, Select produces the control list. Those are the next two workbooks.

## The one idea to take away

The RMF is not a checklist someone invented to slow you down. It is the discipline that lets a real, accountable person put their name to "this system's risk is acceptable." Every step exists so that signature means something.
